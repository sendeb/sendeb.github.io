Get to Know Me

I'm a software engineer at Qualcomm in San Deigo, CA. I graduate from UC Berkeley in 2017 with a degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. After graduating, I was interested in learning more about a topics that having quite a moment in the tech world. Any guesses? That's right! Machine Learning! While completing my masters at UCLA, I spent 5 months doing research on deep learning methods for automated image segmentation, wrote a thesis, and even contributed to a paper publication! With a strong understading in machine learning, I decided to switch gears completely and embarked on a jounrey to learn about a completely different topic: wireless technology and embedded systems. At Qualcomm, I'm currenrly working on project collaborating with JPL to build the next generation space platform. Although I work with embedded systems now, I still actively continue research in computer vision and deep learning and hope to find a way to maybe even combine these two branches of computer science one day!

Enough about the technical talk: on my free time you can catch me at the beach or exploring the next best sunday brunch location with friends and family. When descirbing my palate, I would say it's anything but ordinary: I'm talking earl grey macarons and honey lavendar flavored anything! I've been bit by the travel bug and my goal has been to visit one new place every year. In summary, I'm an inquisitive soul that still craves to explore, learn and discover.
